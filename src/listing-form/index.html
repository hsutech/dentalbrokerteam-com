Listing Form

This page preserves the old listing-form URL and explains what information a seller should prepare before listing a dental practice. Useful details include practice location, production, collections, provider mix, hygiene schedule, lease terms, equipment, staff structure, reason for transition and desired timeline.

The form itself should be connected to the final CRM or submission endpoint before production. For now, contact the team directly before sending confidential practice information.
