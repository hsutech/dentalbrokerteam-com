About Dental Broker Team

Dentist-led brokerage, valuation and transition support for Ontario dental practices.

Coach. Dentist. Broker.

Dental Broker Team is positioned for dentists who want guidance that understands both the economics and the clinical reality of practice ownership. The work is organized around sellers, appraisals, buyers and confidential listings.

The goal is not to make every practice look the same. It is to help owners and buyers understand what creates value, what weakens it and what should be prepared before a serious conversation begins.

How the team helps

The site supports confidential first conversations, appraisal preparation, buyer readiness, listing review and educational resources for dentists considering ownership or transition decisions.
