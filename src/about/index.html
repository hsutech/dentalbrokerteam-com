Dental Broker Team exists for dentists facing ownership decisions that deserve more than a generic business brokerage process. The archived site highlighted the practical experience behind the brand: dentists who understand what it means to build a practice, operate one, value one and transition one.

That matters because dental practice value is not only a matter of financial statements. It is shaped by provider behavior, patient trust, hygiene systems, team continuity, lease quality, equipment, clinical mix and the transition plan after closing. A broker who understands those details can ask better questions and present the practice more clearly.

This rebuilt site keeps the core service structure: selling a dental practice, appraisals, buyers, listings, confidentiality and education. It removes placeholder footer links from the old WordPress build and replaces them with usable pages, schema and complete static content.

Dentist-led practice brokerage, appraisals and transition guidance for Ontario dental practice owners and buyers.
