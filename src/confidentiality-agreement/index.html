Confidentiality Agreement

Confidentiality guidance for dental practice buyers and sellers before sensitive information is shared.

Dental practice transitions require discretion. Staff, patients, competitors and vendors should not learn about a possible sale before the owner chooses the right timing and process.

Before detailed practice information is shared, a buyer may be asked to complete confidentiality documentation and confirm basic acquisition readiness. This protects the seller and helps serious buyers receive the information they need in an organized way.

For the current agreement process, email info@dentalbrokerteam.com or start through Let's Talk.
