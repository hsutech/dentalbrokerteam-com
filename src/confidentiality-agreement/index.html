Confidentiality Agreement

Confidentiality is central to dental practice brokerage. Sellers need to protect staff morale, patient trust, competitive position and financial information. Buyers need enough detail to evaluate an opportunity responsibly. A confidentiality agreement creates the ground rules for sharing sensitive information.

This static page preserves the old confidentiality-agreement URL. The final legal document should be reviewed and supplied by the team before any confidential materials are released.

To request the current agreement, contact info@dentalbrokerteam.com or use the contact page.
